## Notifications

Push notifications keep players connected to their axolotl without being intrusive. All notifications should feel caring rather than alarming. Notification frequency and wording should be warm and gentle — players should feel reminded, not nagged.

All notifications are configurable in Settings. Players can toggle categories on or off.

## Notification Principles

- **Never notify more than twice per day** for the same axolotl’s wellbeing
- **Never notify during quiet hours** — respect system Do Not Disturb settings
- **Sacred moments are notification-free** — rebirth, breeding reveal, egg hatch should never be interrupted by an unrelated notification
- **Tone is warm and caring** — notifications feel like a friend reminding you, not a system demanding action
- **Easy to manage** — notification categories clearly labeled in Settings, one-tap to silence any category
