# Currency & Monetization

---

## Overview

Two-currency system. Coins are the free everyday currency earned through normal play. Opals are the premium currency purchasable with real money or earnable slowly through engagement. The free experience is genuinely complete — Opals enhance and accelerate, never gate core mechanics.

---

## Coins

**Type:** Free currency
**Earned by:** Mini games, daily tasks, daily login bonus, daily spin wheel
**Spent on:** Food, basic decorations, filter upgrades, standard shop items, treatment items

Coins are the lifeblood of daily play. Players who engage consistently will always have enough Coins for their basic care needs. The economy should never feel scarce for an active player.

---

## Opals

**Type:** Premium currency
**Purchased with:** Real money via in-app purchase
**Also earned through:** Rewarded ads, spin wheel, achievements, login streaks, exceptional mini game performance

Opals buy things that feel special — rare variants, exclusive cosmetics, convenience skips, and extra capacity. Nothing purchased with Opals is required to have a complete and satisfying experience.

---

## Opal Earn Rates for Free Players

Free players should be able to earn one meaningful premium purchase approximately every 1–2 weeks of consistent daily play.

| Source | Opals Earned | Frequency |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Rewarded video ad | 2–3 Opals | Up to 5x per day |
| Daily spin wheel (Opal outcome) | 5–10 Opals | ~1 in 7 spins |
| Achievement milestone | 1-2 Opals | One time each |
| Login streak bonus | 15–20 Opals | Days 7, 30, 100 |
| Exceptional mini game score | 3 Opals | Occasional drop |

## Rewarded Ads

Rewarded ads are the primary free Opal earn mechanism. Always opt-in — player taps a clearly labeled button to watch a 30-second ad and receive Opals. Never forced, never interruptive.

**Rules:**

- Maximum 5 rewarded ads per day
- Player initiates every time — no auto-play
- Sacred emotional moments are ad-free — rebirth ceremony, breeding reveals, egg hatching
- Ad button never appears during gameplay, only on menus and between sessions

The players who watch ads and earn Opals slowly are worth more than frustrated players who quit. Opt-in rewarded ads build goodwill; forced ads destroy it.

---

## Daily Spin Wheel

One free spin per day. Resets at midnight. Separate from the daily login bonus.

**Wheel composition:**

- Most outcomes — small Bubble rewards (keeps it feeling fair)
- Occasional outcomes — medium Bubble rewards
- Rare outcomes — small Opal reward (5–10 Opals)
- Very rare outcomes — meaningful Opal reward or exclusive cosmetic item

The spin wheel is a daily retention hook. The anticipation of the rare outcome keeps players opening the app even on low-engagement days.

---

## Daily Login Bonus

Small Bubble reward for opening the app each day. Separate from the spin wheel. Builds the daily open habit without requiring a full play session.

Login streak milestones reward Opals:

- Day 7 streak — 15 Opals
- Day 30 streak — 20 Opals
- Day 100 streak — 20 Opals + exclusive decoration

## What Is Never Paywalled

The following are always free and never require Opals:

- All wellbeing mechanics — feeding, cleaning, water changes
- All life stages including elder and rebirth
- Breeding mechanic
- All mini games
- Friend system and stat comparisons
- Basic decoration and color selection
- Achievement system
- Core stat progression

Charging for any of these would undermine the emotional core of the game and drive casual players away.

---

## Monetization Philosophy

The free experience is genuinely complete and emotionally satisfying. Opals are a shortcut or an exclusive — never a requirement. Players who spend do so because they love the game, not because they feel squeezed.

**Ad experience principles:**

- Always opt-in rewarded format only
- Never interrupt gameplay or emotional moments
- Cap daily ad views to prevent exploitation
- Ad button clearly labeled, never disguised

**Spending experience principles:**

- Premium content is visually exciting and desirable
- Seasonal items create natural urgency without permanently locking content
- Some items purchasable only — not earnable through ads — to give spending unique value
- Confirmation screens on all purchases

## Revenue Projections (Reference)

At 500 genuinely engaged daily active users:

- Ad revenue estimate: ~$300/month
- IAP revenue estimate: ~$70/month
- Total: ~$370/month gross

Backend costs at this scale: $0–25/month (Supabase free tier sufficient).

These figures are starting points. Revenue scales with DAU — 5,000 DAU is approximately 10x these figures.
