# Wellbeing & Stats

---

## Overview

Two distinct stat layers. **Wellbeing stats** represent daily health and decay constantly, requiring player attention. **Character stats** represent long-term progression and build through leveling up. 

---

## Wellbeing Stats

Four stats that decay over real-world time, including when the app is closed.

### Hunger

Primary daily task. Fed with blood worm treats. Decays fastest of the four wellbeing stats. Heavy decay in Baby stage, normal in Adult, reduced in Elder.

### Happiness

Maintained through play and environmental quality. Affected passively by tank decorations with happiness bonuses. Decays at medium rate.

### Cleanliness

Maintained through poop cleaning. 1 poop every 18 hours is the baseline Decays at medium rate when poop present. Additional poops for every 4 worms fed.

### Water Quality

Slowest decaying stat. Acts as a passive multiplier on all three other wellbeing stats. Managed through water changes and filter tier. Higher filter tier slows decay rate significantly. Poops left for a significant amount of time increase decay

## Stat Relationships

Water Quality acts as a passive multiplier on all other wellbeing stats. Poor water quality accelerates the decay of Hunger, Happiness, and Cleanliness simultaneously. Good water quality slightly slows their decay.

**Happiness is influenced by:**

- Active play
- Tank decoration quality
- Cleanliness level
- Water quality
- Life stage appropriate care

**Cleanliness is influenced by:**

- Player cleaning tasks
- Water quality

## Neglect Progression

Four stages of neglect. Recovery is always possible, but consequences remain.

### Stage 1 — Neglected

One or more wellbeing stats in decline. Axolotl looks visibly sad and lethargic. Push notification sent. Easy to recover with normal care. No lasting consequence.

### Stage 2 — Struggling

Prolonged neglect across multiple stats. Axolotl visibly sick with visual indicators on character. Recovery possible with focused care. No lasting consequence if caught at this stage.

### Stage 3 — Critical

Extended severe neglect. Recovery requires significant effort and rare treatment items purchasable with Bubbles or occasionally dropped from mini games. Still recoverable. temporary character stat decline

### Stage 4 — Consequence

Severe neglect that reaches Critical and remains there may permanently cap one character stat slightly lower or delay the life stage XP gain rate. Not devastating but noticeable. Rewards players who maintain consistent care without catastrophically punishing casual ones.

## Character Stats

Four stats that build over time through leveling up. These are the stats players compare with friends.

### ⚡ Stamina

Endurance and overall health capacity. Higher Stamina means wellbeing stats decay slightly slower. 

### 💪 Strength

Physical power. Influences performance in Treasure Hunt mini game. 

### 🏃 Speed

Quickness and reaction. Influences performance in Flappy Fish Hooks and Keeper Upey. 

### 🧠 Intellect

Mental sharpness. Influences performance in Axolotl Stacker and Math Game. Adds bonus chance for higher rarity egg when breading or rebirthing.

## Character Stat Mechanics

**How stats build**
Gaining levels is how character stats increase. 

Level up stat rewards

- Levels 1-9- Every time a level is gained a player gets 1 attribute points they get to apply to a stat as a permanent increase.
- Levels 10-19- Every time a level is gained a player gets 1 attribute points they get to apply to a stat as a permanent increase.
- Levels 20-29- Every time a level is gained a player gets 2 attribute points they get to apply to a stat as a permanent increase.
- Levels 30-40- Every time a level is gained a player gets 2 attribute points they get to apply to a stat as a permanent increase.

**Lineage bonus**
Each generation inherits a small starting boost to one or two stats from the parent axolotl. By generation 3–4 these accumulated bonuses become meaningfully noticeable.

## XP Bar and Stages—

XP is primarily gained through mini games with wellbeing tasks being secondary

**Fills through:**

- Mini game play — highest XP per activity
- Care tasks — very small XP
- Exceptional mini game performance — bonus XP

**Level and XP amount required**

Level 1 to achieve level 2 is 10 XP. Every subsequent level goes up by 5

Stages

- Baby- Levels 1-9
- Juvenile levels 10-19
- Adult- levels 20-29
- Elder- levels 30-40

Once level 40 is reached, Rebirth becomes available as a player choice.
